**🎯 Purpose**

- Built for part-time stocktaking employees who work on irregular schedules. Quickly log which days you worked, whether it was an in-city or out-of-city shift, track custom pay rates, and view your monthly earnings at a glance.


---


**✨ Features**

- **Monthly Calendar** — Visual grid with color-coded work days (cyan for in-city, orange for out-of-city).

- **Quick Day Entry** — Tap any day to log a shift via a smooth bottom sheet form.

- **Auto-Fill Payments** — Default rates are auto-filled based on shift type; editable for double-pay days.

- **Notes** — Add optional notes per day (city visited, details, etc.).

- **Earnings Summary** — Total work days and total earnings displayed below the calendar.

- **Month Navigation** — Swipe through months with arrow buttons; tap the month name to jump to today.

- **Remembers Last View** — App reopens on the last month you were viewing.

- **Settings** — Configure default in-city / out-of-city pay rates.

- **Bilingual** — Full Turkish and English language support, switchable in settings.

- **Data Management** — Option to delete all data from settings.

- **Persistent Storage** — All data saved locally as JSON; survives app restarts.
